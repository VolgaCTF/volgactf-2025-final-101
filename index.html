Where am I?

If you are reading this, chances are, you have made it to VolgaCTF 2025 Final. Congratulations!

What should I do?

Get a vulnbox, then launch it on one of your team's computers and connect it to the network via a bridged interface. Set static IP to 10.5.1XY.3 where XY stands for your team number (zero-based). Play CTF!

What is my team number?

Team numbers will be made available in Telegram chat. Besides, they will be written down on tables at the venue.

I have a problem...

Should you run into any technical difficulties, please don't hesitate to address the organiser's team in Telegram chat.
